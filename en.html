  • Site of health space Shanti_Space

    The task is: create a site-business card of space for various areas of training, blogging with useful articles on the topic of health, user registration, interaction with users.

  • Online store of balloon compositions

    The task is: develop an online store by holiday categories.

    Done: turnkey site (from design to optimization for search engines). The project was implemented using Next js. Adaptive and schematic layout. A catalog of compositions on the theme of the holiday has been implemented. On each page there is a sorting of compositions by price and a filter by feature. For the convenience of the user, a page of favorite products has been developed, with the possibility of adding to the cart. You can adjust your purchases in the shopping cart (add/subtract or delete compositions). The keyword search page has been implemented. Database development and connection. The administrator accepts orders and adjusts compositions on a separate site, which is built on React js. After processing the order by the administrator, the customer receives an email with order details (made a layout of an email).

    Technologies: frontend - Next js; backend - Node js, Express, Mongoose, Sendgrid; admin - React, Formik, Redux (redux-thunk), SASS, MUI, axios.
